10% ROI Guarantee Framework

Structured as a controlled,
reserve-backed target.

Guarantee Architecture

  • Offer only to qualifying units following One Homes Collection standards.
  • Calculate return annually after deducting operating costs and management fee.
  • Return Support Reserve funded from a portion of management fee and setup margin.
  • If actual owner net return is below 10%, approved shortfall is topped up from reserve.
  • Sky villas and owner-blocked units require separate underwriting.

Essential Exclusions

  • Owner blocks peak dates or reduces annual availability below qualifying threshold.
  • Owner refuses approved upgrades or standard operating requirements.
  • Regulatory change, force majeure, building closure or major maintenance shutdown.
  • Damage or unusual repair cost outside normal operations.
  • Early termination before the annual guarantee period is completed.
