Revenue Streams

1. Setup Margins: Every unit requires furniture, electronics, and professional photography. We split this margin with owners.

2. Management Fees: We charge a management fee (25%) on recurring revenue, turning units into income-generating assets.

3. Staff Utilization: By leveraging existing building staff (housekeeping/security), we subsidize building operations while maintaining high service levels.
