10% ROI Guarantee Framework

Structured as a controlled,
reserve-backed target.

Guarantee Architecture

  • Offer only to qualifying units following One Homes Collection furnishing and availability standards.
  • Calculate return annually, not monthly, after deducting eligible operating costs and management fee.
  • Create a Return Support Reserve funded from a portion of management fee and setup margin.
  • If actual owner net return is below 10%, the approved shortfall is topped up from the reserve, subject to exclusions.
  • Large sky villas and owner-blocked units require separate underwriting.

Essential Exclusions

  • Owner blocks peak dates or reduces annual availability below the qualifying threshold.
  • Owner refuses approved upgrades, replacement items or standard operating requirements.
  • Regulatory change, platform restriction, force majeure, building closure or major maintenance shutdown.
  • Damage, misconduct or unusual repair cost outside normal operations.
  • Early termination before the annual guarantee period is completed.
Director decision: Do not market "guaranteed 10%" until finance, legal, reserve, exclusions and eligible-unit criteria are approved.
